Who We Are

{{ CHURCH_NAME }}—also known as Shalom, Shalom —is a Christ-centered, Bible-believing Sabbath church devoted to worship, discipleship, prayer, and compassionate service. We exist to glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurturing believers into mature disciples, and demonstrating God’s love in practical ways within our communities.

Our congregation gathers for vibrant Sabbath worship marked by heartfelt praise, Spirit-led prayer, and Bible exposition that is faithful, clear, and applicable. We place a strong emphasis on spiritual formation— equipping children, youth, and adults to follow Jesus in everyday life, honor God in work and family, and live as ambassadors of the Kingdom in their neighborhoods and nations.

Leadership and Shepherding

Under the apostolic leadership of Apostle Ephraim Kwaku Danso, our church is shepherded by a dedicated team of ministers and lay leaders who model servant leadership, biblical integrity, and pastoral care. We believe leadership is a stewardship to equip the saints for the work of ministry, cultivate healthy families, and raise future leaders who will serve the Church and society with wisdom and grace.

Compassionate Outreach

Compassion is central to our identity. Through annual charity initiatives and ongoing benevolence programs, we mobilize resources, volunteers, and partnerships to support orphans, widows, persons with disabilities, and vulnerable families. Our outreach includes practical relief, education support, medical and accessibility aid, and mentoring. We serve not to be seen, but so that Christ would be seen through tangible love.

What We Value

We value the authority of Scripture, the power of prayer, authentic community, holiness of life, excellence in service, and mission to the world. As a multi-generational family, we celebrate cultural diversity and unity in Christ. We welcome newcomers, create pathways for belonging and growth, and encourage every believer to discover and deploy their God-given gifts.

Whether you are exploring faith, returning to church, or seeking a spiritual home for your family, we invite you to worship with us, get connected, and join us in God’s mission. Together, we pursue Jesus, build one another up in love, and serve our communities with grace and truth.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love others, and serve the world through the power of the Holy Spirit, spreading the Gospel to all nations.

Our Vision

To be a thriving, multi-generational church family that transforms lives and communities through authentic worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate service.

Our Values

Faith, Hope, Love, Community, Service, and Excellence in all we do, guided by Scripture and empowered by the Holy Spirit.
