Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Here are answers to some common questions first-time visitors have about joining our church family.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just bring yourself! We provide Bibles, and you don't need to bring anything else. If you prefer to use your own Bible or take notes, feel free to bring those. We want your first visit to be as comfortable as possible.

What about my children?

Children are always welcome in our main service! We also offer age-appropriate programs during worship: nursery (0-2), children's church (3-12), and youth group (13-18). All our children's workers are background-checked volunteers who love serving families.

Will I be asked to stand up or participate?

We may invite visitors to raise their hand so we can welcome you, but there's absolutely no pressure to participate in anything you're not comfortable with. Feel free to observe and participate at your own pace - we want you to feel completely at ease.

What if I arrive late?

No problem at all! Our friendly greeters will help you find a seat even if you arrive after the service has started. We understand that life happens, and you're welcome whenever you arrive. Grace extends to timing too!
