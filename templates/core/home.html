Our Foundation

What We Believe In

Our faith is anchored in timeless biblical truths that transform lives and build lasting community.

Scripture Authority

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word, our ultimate authority for faith, life, and spiritual truth.

"All Scripture is God-breathed" - 2 Timothy 3:16

Salvation by Grace

We believe salvation is a free gift from God through faith in Jesus Christ, not by our works.

"For by grace you have been saved" - Ephesians 2:8

Christian Community

We believe in the power of authentic fellowship, mutual support, and growing together in faith.

"Bear one another's burdens" - Galatians 6:2
